Question 1

Description

Question 1 is divided into two parts, each worth two marks. These questions tend to focus on your understanding of the vocabulary list in the case study The questions typically require you to provide a definition of the term.

Sample Questions

  • Define Platform as a Service (Paas)
  • Describe what is meant by behavioral data.
  • Define the term precision as it relates to recommender sytenms.

How to Respond

To get the two marks, you need to provide two points. One mark is to give the definition and one mark is to provide an elaboration or clear example.

Question 2

Description

This question is four marks and requires you to make some application of some concept in the case study.

Sample Questions

  • Outline the steps of the K-nearest neighbor algorithm in determining new content.
  • Outline the steps of the root mean square error (RMSE) approach in evaluating recommandor systems.

How to Respond

  • Each step should be clearly shown
  • One step per line
  • Use bullet points
  • Each step should be atomic.

Question 3

Description

This question is for six marks and requires an extended response. Typically, the exam will require you to either explain a concept in detail or evaluate the pros and cons of two related concepts in the case study. One other favorite exam question is to compare and contrast two concepts.

There is not enough material in the case study for you to answer these questions. You must research the content yourself.

Sample Questions

  • Compare and contrast the k-NN algorithm and the matrix factorization algorithm in filtering content.
  • Outline two ethical concerns when using user data in recommender systems.
  • Explain how training data influences the quality of a recommender system.
  • Compare and contrast unservised learning and reinforcement learning .

How to Respond

To get full marks on an explnation, stick to "subject verb object" sentences - simple and clear. Use connecting phrases such as "that means..." or "for example" to show the examiners that you know what you are talking about.

Question 4

Description

This question for 12 marks is the hardest question and will require the most time. You will be given a prompt and expected to write an extended essay to answer it. The essay is designed to be open ended without a clear right or wrong in which you are given a context and a statement and asked to evaluate the validity of that statement.

Sample Questions

Alice and Bob are discussing the implementation of the NextStar system with potential investors. Some investors are concerned about the use of user data, in terms of both security and accuracy. Are the investors' concerns justified? In an extended response, explain how Alice and Bob might address the concerns of the investors.

To what extent can the developers in charge of implementing the NextStar system be confident in the accuracy of its recommndations?

How to Respond

To get the best mark, you must show that you have undertaken independant research and show that you can connect the concepts in the case study to the concepts covered in the course. Use heading titles in your response to show the examiner how your response is strucuted. In addition, you must have several examples ready to go to use in your response.
