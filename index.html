Master the Exam

Mastering a test is more than just memorizing a bunch of facts. Knowing how the exam is strucutred and what the examiners are expecting from you is crucial.

2023 Exams at a glance.

Paper 1

Date: May 5th, 2023

Marks: 100

Duration: 130 minutes

Weighting: 40%

Consists of written response questions in two mandatory sections. Questions tend to integrate several topics. Usually ends with algorithm problems involving pseudocode.

Paper 2

Date: May 8th, 2023

Marks: 65

Duration: 80 minutes

Weighting: 20

Short answer questions about the option topic you covered in class. Most students choose Option D: Object Oriented Programming.

Paper 3

Date: May 8th, 2023

Marks: 30

Duration: 60 minutes

Weighting: 30

Written response questions centered around the Case Study capped with a written essay. This paper is showing that you did the research and understand how the topics in the case study relate to the rest of course topics.
